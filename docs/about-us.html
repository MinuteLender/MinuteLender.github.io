Our History

Our team formed in 2012 to capitalize on the newly available utility-based cloud solutions that enable financial institutions to serve their customers with digital technologies.

We all share deep experience in providing technology platforms to financial institutions, having worked together in previous companies we founded and sold, and in financial organizations.​
Our Capabilities

Our team brings extensive experience in financial institution technology, data security, operations, marketing, sales, underwriting, risk management, and compliance to provide a turn-key digital solution for our clients.

We provide comprehensive vendor due-diligence documentation, thorough compliance review and independent SOC2/Type2 audit of our security and technology. Our design and configuration has no customer-identifying information stored out side your firewall and full "encryption at rest" of our data.
